About DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO

Program Overview

DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is a family-level Performance Contract system that enables households to define shared commitments, track achievements, and recognize individual contributions.

Kibungo Sector Initiative

This system supports Kibungo Sector’s development goals by providing a structured framework for accountability, performance tracking, and family empowerment.

System Features

Family Information

Centralized registration and management of all family members and household data.

Individual Performance Duties

Each family member is assigned duties that contribute to the overall family Performance Contract (Imihigo).

Shared Family Goals

Define collective goals with shared responsibility and measurable outcomes.

How to Use the System

Access the system directly or with help from an Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga or your local administrative authority.

Register your family and record details for every family member.

Define individual duties and shared family performance goals (Imihigo).

Monitor progress, update achievements, and evaluate family performance.
